Unofficial course review site for Georgia Tech's OMSCS and OMSA Programs

What is this app?

This app was originally created for GT OMSCS students to get a feel for course difficulty and workload.

It is not endorsed or sponsored by the official OMSCS program or staff.

 Who maintains this app?

Matt Martz

 How do I publish a review?

After signing in, click the Write a Review button.

 I am concerned about privacy. How is my information kept secure? Privacy Policy

The app records only your name, email, profile image URL, and OMSCS specialization (if set).

For now... all reviews are shown anonymously (to save on bandwidth). We may go back to the toggle-anonymity in the future (which will be opt-in).

Firebase's security infrastructure guarantees that this information is accessible only to individuals who are registered and authenticated.

 Need to report an issue or an enhancement request?
